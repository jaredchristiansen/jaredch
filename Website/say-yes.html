SayYes App

2017 ∙ School Project

This project revolves around the overwhelming stress that comes from getting in over your head. I’ve recently found myself agreeing to too many commitments, because I don't want to miss out on opportunities. I always say “yes” to everything, but doing so too much becomes overwhelming. This project is a way of expressing those feelings. I have created an app, coded in swift, that launches messages down the screen. Your character is the yellow emoji, and you have to "say yes" to the questions. Let one by, and you lose a life! That is, until your stress bar fills up. Then you have to avoid the messages until it returns to normal. The game gets faster and faster until you lose!
